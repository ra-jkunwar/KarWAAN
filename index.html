About Kaarwaa.N...

Kaarwaa.N... is a non-government orgranisation founded by Jabalpur Engineering College (JEC), Jabalpur students who are committed to work for poor, underprivileged, downtrodden and unaware, with the primary aim to teach students for JNV(Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya) Entrance Exam and promote ‘girls education’. Through different activities we try to address different issues like education, health and hygiene, environmental issues, government scheme and plans, livelihood and community issues.

It is our empathy, the realisation that being citizens of this country, it is our responsibility to take the step for the welfare of another India.

Impact Of Kaarwaa.N...

  • More than thousand children provided education so far through Kaarwaa.N...

  • Many of them knocked the doors of government aided institutions like NAVODAYA and
    GYANODAYA, which will lead to pull up their families from generations of marginalization.

  • Kaarwaa.N has founded a park named as Kaarwaa.N's Garden.

  • Continuous efforts are dedicated for the welfare of minors in orphanages.

  • Regular awareness events on cleanliness, environment and social issues and welfare had illuminated many minds.
